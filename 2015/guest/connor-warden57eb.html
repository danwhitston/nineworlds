Connor Warden

Academic witchcraft specialist

Connor Warden is an historian by training, demonologist by specialism and actor by hobby. He has yet to publish anything of substantive length but is working on an e-book for release, tentatively titled "Piety Through Ordeal: Demonic Possession of Religious Leaders in Early Modern Europe"; he is also currently working on a thesis on witch-hunting at the University of Oxford. Outside of academia, his interests are Poker, roleplaying games and B-movies. If found around the convention and not otherwise rushed off his feet, he loves meeting new people, so please feel free to chat.
