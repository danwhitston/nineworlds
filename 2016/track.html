What's on at Nine Worlds 2016

Our ongoing quest to overthink, overtalk, and overdo everything is taking a slight left turn this year. We've grouped things together so it's easier to see what's going on. There are dozens of subject areas inside the bigger categories, and we'll still be running 250 different talks, performances, workshops, activities and panels across the three days of Nine Worlds.

Academia and Humanities
Crafting and Creating - Writing, Poetry, Art, Fancrafts
Entertainments and parties
Fanworks
Gaming - Video, Tabletop, Social
Identity & Culture - LGBTQAI+, Race, Feminism, Disability
Kids at Nine Worlds
Living Words - Books and Comics
Moving Pictures - Films, TV & Criticism
Socialising and meetups
Technology for People
