Entertainments and parties

Panels and workshops are awesome, but sometimes it's nice to just sit back and take in a show, or have a bit of a party. In addition to a number of track specific events, there are some central events being put on for the attendees of Nine Worlds.

Thursday

  • Cheese and Cheese
    Bordeaux 7pm-10pm
    Ruth Ball
    Cheesy Cheese, Cheesy readings, the perfect combination of cheese and cheese. Also crackers.


  • Ice Breaker Quiz
    Bourg 8pm-10pm
    Rebecca Slater
    A relaxed quiz to start off the convention, with prizes to be won and fun to be had!


  • Thursday Midnight Movie: Dracula A.D. 1972
    Muscadet 12am-2am
    Join us for a screening of Dracula A.D. 1972 starring Christopher Lee, Peter Cushing and Stephanie Beacham. (96 mins)


Friday

  • The Great Arcade
    Cremant 1:30-4:15pm
    For a brief, glorious few hours on Friday afternoon, Cremant (the Great Hall of Nine Worlds) will become a vast space hosting games of all kinds, for all kinds. From parlour and paper games to multiplayer button mashing, we’ll have all sorts to bring out your playful side! Join our crowdsourced Hearthstone run, take part in our Click Clack Lumberjack tournament, play plenty of solo and group games, or do your own thing in our comfy handheld hangout zone - game on!


  • Steven Universe Singalong
    Bourg 6:45pm-7:45pm
    They are the Crystal Gems! They always save the day! And if you want to sing, just come and find the way! Sing along with all of the classic Steven Universe songs such as Strong in the Real Way, Comet, and Stronger Than You!


  • Ready, Steady, FLASH!
    Bouzy 6:45pm-7:45pm
    Lee Harris, Laurie Penny, Cassandra Khaw, Stark Holborn, Paul Cornell
    Everyone loves a good story. But sometimes, inspiration doesn’t strike. Join host Lee Harris and his victims… *ahem* motley crew of professional authors as they attempt to write four pieces of flash fiction. The catch? They don’t know the theme of their stories until a second before they have to start writing them, and they only have five minute in which to write each one! You, the audience, get to decide the winner!


  • The Mechanisms
    Cremant 6:45pm-7:45pm
    We’re very glad to welcome the Mechanisms back to Nine Worlds. Not only will they be performing as part of your Friday night ents, they’ll be bringing a new story with them called ‘the Bifröst incident’.


  • Helen Arney: Voice of an Angle
    Cremant 8:30pm-9:30pm
    Helen is a physicist, presenter, stand-up comedian and musician. Having appeared across the globe we’re very happy to welcome her to Nine Worlds where she’ll be performing as the headline act of Friday night bringing you songs, science, silliness and stand-up. Suitable for ages 14+


  • Video Game Burlesque: Show Me Your Moves! (18+)
    Cremant 10pm-11pm
    Join Lady Stardust for an advneture into the ultimate combo: video games and burlesque. Expect interactive games, Pokemon Go-Go dancers, and a half-naked Link. Performances from Marquissa Dark, Ray Rustle, Fanny Darling and Fey au Lait.


  • Live Commentary for a MYSTERY MOVIE
    Muscadet 10pm-11:45pm
    Andrew Clarke, Kath Iwinski, Luca Saitta
    Join us as we do a live commentary for a MYSTERY MOVIE. We promise it will be thoroughly trashy and thoroughly wonderful. We’ll have Andrew, Kat and Luca leading it, but we’ll be passing the mics around to anyone that wants a crack at making interesting insights/obscure anecdotes/stupid jokes about the movie, and we hope a fun time will be had by all. This totally isn’t just MST3K. Totes. Join us!


  • The Friday Night Geek Rave
    Cremant 11:15pm-2am
    Returning to Nine Worlds, DJ Lum will help you dance the night away with the finest (and cheesiest) of geeky rave music.


  • Friday Midnight Movie: Aliens
    Muscadet 12am-2:30am
    Join us for a screening of Aliens, starring Sigourney Weaver. (137 minutes).


Saturday

  • Swordpunk 1 (Ticketed)
    Bourg 10am-11am
    Swordpunk is coming to Nine Worlds! On Saturday 13th of August, Swordpunk and Nine Worlds present a series of 1 hour workshops in which you will get an introduction to medieval swordsmanship. Whether you’re experienced in other forms of edged weapon combat or new to the entire realm, Swordpunk are all about inclusion, so no previous relevant experience is required.

  • Swordpunk 2 (Ticketed)
    Bourg 11:45am-12:45pm
    Swordpunk is coming to Nine Worlds! On Saturday 13th of August, Swordpunk and Nine Worlds present a series of 1 hour workshops in which you will get an introduction to medieval swordsmanship. Whether you’re experienced in other forms of edged weapon combat or new to the entire realm, Swordpunk are all about inclusion, so no previous relevant experience is required.

  • Swordpunk 3 (Ticketed)
    Bourg 3:15pm-4:15pm
    Swordpunk is coming to Nine Worlds! On Saturday 13th of August, Swordpunk and Nine Worlds present a series of 1 hour workshops in which you will get an introduction to medieval swordsmanship. Whether you’re experienced in other forms of edged weapon combat or new to the entire realm, Swordpunk are all about inclusion, so no previous relevant experience is required.

  • Swordpunk 4 (Ticketed)
    Bourg 5pm-6pm
    Swordpunk is coming to Nine Worlds! On Saturday 13th of August, Swordpunk and Nine Worlds present a series of 1 hour workshops in which you will get an introduction to medieval swordsmanship. Whether you’re experienced in other forms of edged weapon combat or new to the entire realm, Swordpunk are all about inclusion, so no previous relevant experience is required.

  • Something To Sing About: The Whedon Singalong
    Cremant 5pm-6pm
    Sing along with all your favourite tunes from the Whedonverse, featuring Dr Horrible’s Singalong blog, the Buffy musical episode Once More With Feeling, and Firefly. Dancing demons optional.


  • Bifröst - The Cabaret and Disco
    Cremant 7pm-9:30pm (Cabaret) 10:15pm-2am (Disco)
    Bifröst is back and it's bigger and better than ever. Bringing you dexterous dancers, marvellous magicians, commendable comedians, delightful DJs, peerless poets and a congenial compère. Join us for our community cabaret and the after party disco, you won't regret it.


  • Dead By Dawn with Evil Dead 2 (18+)
    Bourg 10:15pm-2am
    There’s something… out there… IN THE WOODS! Which should give you even more reason to come join us in Bourg for some late night entertainment. Preceded by a mixture of weird and wonderful videos and trailers, this year Nine Worlds will be screening the classic EVIL DEAD 2 as its Saturday Midnight Movie! Will you survive, or will you be dead by dawn?


Sunday

  • End of the Con Quiz
    Bourg 5pm-6:45pm
    It's time to wrap things up with the End of the Con Quiz, with more prizes to be won and a chance to cap off your weekend on a high note.
